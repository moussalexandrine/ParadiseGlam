Here are some common questions about our event rental services


  • Do you require a Deposit to book the date?

    We place our customers at the forefront of our business, and to ensure your reservation is secure, we kindly request a 30% retainer as a deposit, which is non-refundable. This allows us to reserve and hold your spot exclusively for your event. The remaining balance is due 42 hours prior to the start of your event, allowing us ample time to prepare and deliver an exceptional experience for you.

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    In the event of a cancellation, we understand unforeseen circumstances may arise, and we are here to assist you. We are committed to finding an available alternative date that suits your needs and is not already booked. However, please note that the retainer paid upon booking is non-refundable. We are more than willing to work closely with you to reschedule your event to an open date that accommodates your preferences and ensures a memorable experience..

  • What types of events do you cater to?

    We cater to a wide range of events, including weddings, corporate events, parties, and more. Whether you're planning a small intimate gathering or a large-scale celebration, we have the rental items to meet your needs.

  • Do you offer delivery and setup?

    Yes, we offer delivery and setup services to ensure a hassle-free experience for our customers. Our professional team will handle the transportation and setup of the rental items at your event venue, allowing you to focus on other aspects of your event. Please note that delivery and setup are two different additional fees.

  • What happens if there is damage to the rental items?

    We understand that accidents can happen. In the case of damage to the rental items, we will refer to the signed contract of the items you rent to determine the course of action. We will assess the situation and work with you to find a fair solution, which may involve repair costs or replacement charges.

  • Can I make changes to my rental order?

    Yes, you can make changes to your rental order up to a certain point. We recommend contacting our team as soon as possible to discuss any modifications or additions you would like to make. We will do our best to accommodate your requests. Cancellation of the event is NOT AN ACCEPTABLE Change!!!
